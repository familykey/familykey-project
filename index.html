Family Key 方案介绍
Solution from Family Key

基于智能合约钱包和抽象账户

  • 1. 以 Safe 智能账户为载体，接入自研 Dead Man’s Switch 模块。
  • 2. 委托人定期重启心跳；心跳终止后受益人获得智能合约钱包权限。
  • 3. 受益人用邮箱/手机登录（Privy/Particle），零加密门槛，自动生成自托管钱包。
  • 4. 通过邀请、到期提醒（Email/SMS/Push）使产品人性化。

Solution

  • 1. Use Safe smart account as the base, integrating our Dead Man’s Switch module.
  • 2. Owner periodically refreshes heartbeat; once it stops, beneficiary gains smart wallet permissions.
  • 3. Beneficiary logs in via email/phone (Privy/Particle); zero crypto barrier; auto-generated self-custody wallet.
  • 4. Humanized experience with invites and expiry reminders (Email/SMS/Push).

核心接口与时序

  1. checkIn()：Owner 刷新心跳（同时清除 claimReadyAt）。
  2. startClaim()：Beneficiary 触发挑战期，设置 claimReadyAt。
  3. finalizeClaim()：Beneficiary 执行 Safe swapOwner 完成过户。
  4. status()：读取 lastCheckIn/heartbeatInterval/claimReadyAt.

APIs & Sequence

  1. checkIn(): Owner refresh heartbeat (also clears claimReadyAt).
  2. startClaim(): Beneficiary triggers challenge period and sets claimReadyAt.
  3. finalizeClaim(): Beneficiary performs Safe swapOwner to transfer ownership.
  4. status(): Read lastCheckIn/heartbeatInterval/claimReadyAt.
